valid:
Did the parser extract anything usable.

target_rank:
Where the target item appears (lower is better).

rr (reciprocal rank):
1 / rank. Rewards top positions more strongly.

hit@1:
Whether the target item is ranked first.

hit@5:
Whether the target item is in the top 5.

coverage:
How much of the candidate list was recovered (num_ranked / total_candidates).

rank_delta:
Change in target rank compared to baseline (positive = improvement).

rr_delta:
Change in reciprocal rank vs baseline.

jaccard@5:
Overlap between top-5 items of method and baseline.

pairwise_agreement:
How much of the ranking order stayed consistent vs baseline.
